FM Scaffolding is an Australian owned and operated company. Since our inception in 2001 we have developed a proud reputation of providing quality scaffolding hire services to business large and small.

Complete Scaffolding Solution

FM Scaffolding provides a complete scaffolding solution. We can assist with your scaffold drawings and supply you with the scaffolding you require, either sales or hire.

Scaffolding Hire.

FM Scaffolding specialise in the Design, Erection and Dismantling of hire Quickstage, modular scaffolding. We also provide Aluminium Scaffolding if required. We provide our scaffolding hire service to all types Commercial Industrial and Domestic customers including
  • Builders
  • Carpenters
  • Painters
  • Brick Layers
  • Owner Builders
