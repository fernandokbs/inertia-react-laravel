Scaffolding Hire Sydney
 

Scaffolding

Our modular scaffolding system is able to cater for all access scaffold requirements. The wedge fixing of the ledgers and transoms gives a simple and fast means of erecting access scaffolding. Conforms to BS1139 Part 5, HD1000, OSHA and Australian New Zealand Standards AS/NZS 1576.1:2010.

FM Scaffolding use only quality scaffolding materials which are maintained with our ongoing maintenance program. All materials returned from a location are inspected, cleaned and repaired where necessary, before being allowed to be used again.

Our experienced and qualified staff will make sure your project runs smoothly, safely, on budget and on time. FM Scaffolding has extensive experience working projects of all sizes so no project is too large.

Scaffolding Hire

Scaffolding can be hired by the week or a long-term contract can be arranged to suit your project. We can also deploy scaffolding rapidly in the case of emergencies resulting from accidents.We also provide Aluminium Scaffolding if required.
